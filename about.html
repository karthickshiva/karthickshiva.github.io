about

I'm Karthick Shiva — engineer, AI practitioner, and recovering mathematician. I design and build AI systems for a living, with a focus on making models that are not just accurate but interpretable, robust, and grounded in first principles.

Mathematics is the lens I use for everything. Whether it's probability theory behind a transformer, the topology of a latent space, or the algebraic structure hiding inside a chord progression — I believe rigorous thinking makes better engineers and better ideas.

Music theory is my other obsession. I find it deeply mathematical: voice leading follows rules of tension and resolution that look a lot like optimisation; harmonic series is just physics; counterpoint is constraint satisfaction. It keeps me honest about the difference between pattern and understanding.

This blog is where all of it meets: AI, engineering, mathematics, and occasionally music. No tracking. No ads. No JavaScript frameworks. Just clear thinking and a stylesheet.
